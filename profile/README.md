## 🌟 About AGTechathon

AGTechathon 2.0 is a national-level hackathon that brings together passionate developers, designers, innovators, and technology enthusiasts to solve real-world challenges through innovation and collaboration.

The event provides a platform for participants to transform creative ideas into impactful solutions while enhancing their technical, problem-solving, and teamwork skills.

## 🎯 Focus Areas

* Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
* Web Development
* Mobile Applications
* Cloud Computing
* Cybersecurity
* Internet of Things (IoT)
* AgriTech & Sustainability
* Open Innovation
